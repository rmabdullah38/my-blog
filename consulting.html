CONSULTING

As a seasoned essay writer and tutor, I'm here to support you in your high school and college admissions journey. I'll help you enhance your essays, making them unique and appealing to colleges. I'll guide you every step of the way, from brainstorming to finalizing your college activities. I have a deep understanding of the essay-writing process, and I'll teach you strategies to stand out to admissions officers. My past students have been accepted into prestigious schools, and I'm confident I can do the same for you.

ABOUT THE LESSON

I like to first get an understanding of where the student is in their comprehension of the concept or idea they're struggling with and then work with what they already know to explain the concept better, and then evaluate them to see if their understanding improved or if we should take a different approach.
