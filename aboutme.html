ABOUT RONNY

Hi, my name is Ronny, and I am a sophomore at Stanford University, majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Math. My academic excellence in high school earned me recognition in the top 1% of my class and as an AP Scholar with Distinction, averaging 5 on 12 AP tests. I've received several scholarships, including the Questbridge National College Match, Jack Kent Cooke College Scholarship, and Burger King, and was a semifinalist for the Elks National MVS Scholarship. With 4+ years of tutoring experience, I've helped students from elementary to high school levels with math, including AP Calculus and standardized tests such as ACT and SAT

As a seasoned essay writer and tutor, I'm here to support you in your college admissions journey. I'll help you enhance your essays, making them unique and appealing to colleges. I'll guide you every step of the way, from brainstorming to finalizing your college activities. I have a deep understanding of the essay-writing process, and I'll teach you strategies to stand out to admissions officers. My past students have been accepted into prestigious schools, and I'm confident I can do the same for you.

In addition to essay writing, I also have experience with standardized test preparation (ACT and SAT). I've helped several students significantly improve their scores, and I'll share my techniques and tips to help you ace these tests.

Tutoring math is a passion of mine as I find joy in helping others succeed in a subject that can be challenging. My one-on-one approach allows for personalized teaching, leading to deeper understanding and better results. I'm dedicated to helping you achieve your goals, so let's get started!
